About Us

Family owned and operated The Rathcormac Inn’s history is steeped with proud Irish culture and lore reflecting the original authentic Irish pub. The atmosphere is warm and welcoming, a place for people to gather and socialize, sing, relax, tell stories, and exchange the latest topics of interest.

There is a wide variety of food on offer including traditional Irish dishes, steaks, curries and salads. Our full bar boasts an extensive whiskey list, with a full range of imported and domestic beers, wine and liquors. We are a Premier Sports Bar with 5 HDTV's and 1 x 100" big screens and virtually every major satellite/cable package available; our friendly staff will cater to your every need and will be able to accommodate any sports request that you have!

Come see the impressive line-up of events on offer from live trad-sessions, line dancing, Aerobics, Bingo, Social Dancing to name a few.

Consider The Rathcormac Inn for your next party, business luncheon or private gathering. We have a spacious function room accommodating parties of up to 200ppl, or our newly built modern and sleek Restaurant for parties of 30-70ppl with party packages that wont be beat.

You can find us on the Dublin Road on the Cork side of Fermoy only 20mins from the City.
